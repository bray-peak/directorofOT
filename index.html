Notes & Fine Print

  • Director's billable hour expectation starts at 27 hrs/week and decreases by 2 hrs for each COTA supervised and 5 hrs for each OT supervised, reflecting increased administrative and supervisory responsibility.
  • Ryan is included in the base $15,000 bonus pool. Each additional COTA adds $2,500 to the pool; each additional OT adds $5,000.
  • COTA performance bonus (55% of pool) is divided equally among all COTAs. The director earns each COTA's proportional share scaled by the percentage of evaluation periods that COTA hit their goal. Example: if Ryan hits his goal 60% of periods, the director earns 60% of Ryan's share.
  • OT performance bonus is divided equally among supervised OTs and earned proportionally the same way.
  • Staff retention bonus (10% of pool) is all-or-nothing: 100% retention for the quarter earns the full amount. Any turnover forfeits this component entirely.
  • Paid time off, paid holidays, and full office closures do not count against any provider's or the director's billable hour expectations. Hours are measured against available working time only.
  • Ramp bonus of $2,500 is a one-time payment earned when the director first meets her billable hour expectation. It does not recur annually.
  • All bonus components are evaluated and paid on a schedule determined by Peak Behavioral Health leadership.
